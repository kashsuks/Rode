About Pinel

Pinel is a fast and efficient code-editor that just works. Meant for developers that want simplicity yet require extreme customization.

Our goal is to provide developers with tools that are just as fast yet customizable as your favourite code-editors without comprising quality.

How it started

Pinel was a project I started as a part of Flavortown a program that rewards teengars with prizes for building cool stuff.

The project was originally Rode (Rust + Code) = Rode however there was already a project with the same name. We (members of Hack Club I knew) came to a consensus that Pinel would be the new name for the project!
